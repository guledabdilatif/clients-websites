Fruits and Vegetables.

RASHCO LIMITED supplies a diverse selection of fresh fruits and vegetable seeds, sourced from trusted suppliers to support agricultural development and food security initiatives. We provide high-quality seeds that are carefully selected for their ability to thrive in local conditions, ensuring optimal yield and sustainability. Our fruits and vegetables offerings cater to both commercial farmers and small-scale growers, promoting healthier communities through access to nutritious produce. With a focus on quality and reliability, RASHCO LIMITED is dedicated to supporting the agricultural sector by delivering seeds that contribute to successful farming and food production.

Food items

RASHCO LIMITED supplies a wide range of high-quality food items, carefully sourced to meet the nutritional needs of diverse communities. Our food products include grains, pulses, canned goods, and other essential staples, ensuring freshness, safety, and affordability. We prioritize timely delivery to ensure that our clients, including humanitarian organizations and local businesses, receive the necessary supplies to support their operations. Whether for relief efforts or commercial use, RASHCO LIMITED is dedicated to providing top-tier food items that meet industry standards for quality and sustainability.

fuel supply

RASHCO LIMITED offers reliable fuel supply services, ensuring uninterrupted energy solutions for businesses, NGOs, and humanitarian projects. We provide high-quality fuel, including diesel and gasoline, sourced from trusted suppliers to meet the demands of various industries. Our efficient distribution network guarantees timely deliveries, even in remote locations, making us a dependable partner for critical operations. Whether for transportation, power generation, or emergency relief, RASHCO LIMITED is committed to delivering affordable and consistent fuel supply to keep your projects running smoothly.

Non-food Item

RASHCO LIMITED provides a comprehensive range of non-food items essential for humanitarian aid, development projects, and community support initiatives. Our offerings include hygiene kits, dignity kits, school kits, WASH items, and other critical non-food supplies aimed at improving living conditions. We also provide shelter materials and emergency relief items for displaced populations or disaster-affected communities. Each product is sourced with quality, durability, and practicality in mind, ensuring that our clients can trust in the reliability of these vital supplies. RASHCO LIMITED is dedicated to supporting well-being and safety through timely and efficient delivery of non-food items.

Construction material.

RASHCO LIMITED supplies high-quality construction materials for a variety of projects, from infrastructure development to emergency shelter construction. Our materials include cement, steel, timber, roofing sheets, and other essential building components, ensuring durability and safety in every project. Whether for commercial, residential, or humanitarian construction, we are committed to delivering reliable and affordable materials that meet industry standards. With our efficient supply chain, RASHCO LIMITED guarantees timely delivery to ensure that construction projects can be completed on schedule, even in remote or challenging locations.

Shelter material.

RASHCO LIMITED provides durable and reliable shelter materials, designed to support emergency response efforts and humanitarian projects. Our range includes tents, tarpaulins, roofing sheets, poles, and other essential items used to construct temporary shelters for displaced populations or those affected by disasters. We ensure that all materials are of high quality, weather-resistant, and easy to assemble, providing quick and effective solutions during crises. RASHCO LIMITED is committed to helping communities rebuild by supplying shelter materials that offer safety, comfort, and protection in the most challenging conditions.
